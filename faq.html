Delivery

Q. When will my order ship?

All in-stock orders received by 12pm PST Monday to Thursday will be shipped out the same day. Orders received after 12pm PST Monday to Wednesday, will be shipped out the following day. Orders received after 12pm on Thursday, will be shipped out on Monday.

Q. How are my products shipped?

They are shipped by BlueDart.

Q. When will my products arrive?

Generally orders take 3-4 days to ship.

Payment

Q. How can I pay for my order?

We accept credit card.

Q. What credit cards do you accept?

We accept VISA, MasterCard, American Express and Discover card.

Q. Is the product in stock?

We strive to maintain sufficient inventory to meet your needs. In the rare case that a product is out of stock, you will be notified at the time of your order. We will then do everything we can to ensure that your order is fulfilled within 30 days. You will not be charged until your order has shipped.

Cancellations and Returns

Q. How do I cancel my order?

If you need to cancel your order please contact us at 1.250.294.9065. Please keep in mind that we strive to process and ship orders the same day (if received before 1pm PST), and once the order has shipped you will be subject to our return policy.

Q. Can I return the product to JustRent?

All returns must have prior authorization from JustRent, and are subject to a 25% restocking fee on the items plus any shipping & handling charges.
